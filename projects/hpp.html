Holonomic Pure Pursuit + Pose Lock

Geometric trajectory tracking with dynamic lookahead and endpoint pose locking to improve repeatability near final approach.

Overview

This controller combines path-following behavior for mid-course motion with a terminal pose-lock mode for precise final alignment in both translation and heading.

What I Built

  • Implemented pure pursuit for holonomic trajectory tracking with arc-length progression.
  • Added dynamic lookahead scaling to balance responsiveness and smoothness.
  • Decoupled heading management from translational tracking when needed.
  • Implemented pose-lock logic near goal to settle residual position and heading error.
  • Applied S-curve modifications to reduce abrupt command transitions.
